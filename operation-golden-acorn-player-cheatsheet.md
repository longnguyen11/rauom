# Operation: Golden Acorn - Player Cheat Sheet

Updated: March 10, 2026 (America/New_York)

## Objective

Earn the **most stars** by the end of the event.

Stars come from:
- Winning challenges
- Discovering secrets
- End-of-week bonuses
- The final star auction

**Stars win the game. `Bitacorn` helps you get stars.**

---

## Daily Flow

**Morning Challenge**
- Team competition
- Awards stars and sometimes `Bitacorn`

**Daily Missions**
- Each team gets several tasks
- Exactly **2 tasks involve discovering another team's secret**
- You may only submit secret claims for those 2 assigned targets

**Impromptu Challenges**
- Surprise mini-games during the day
- Usually reward `Bitacorn`

**Daily Recap**
- 5-minute summary of highlights

---

## Discovering Secrets

Your team can uncover secrets in three ways:

### 1) Social Investigation

Ask questions, observe behavior, and talk to people.

### 2) Trade

Teams may buy/sell secrets using `Bitacorn`.

### 3) 3-Option Fallback (Last Resort)

If negotiations fail, fallback may be triggered.

- Trigger is **captain-to-captain only**
- Defending team provides exactly 3 options
- Exactly 1 option is true
- Your team gets **one guess only**
- That guess becomes your mission answer

---

## Important Twist

Different teams can receive different option sets.

If teams compare notes, they may deduce the real answer together.

Alliances are allowed. Betrayal is also allowed.

---

## Secret Rewards

Correct secret discovery:
- `+2 stars`

If multiple teams chase the same secret:
- First team to submit the correct answer gets `+20 Bitacorn` (one-time per secret)
- Other teams can still complete and earn stars

---

## Bitacorn

Every team starts with **`200 Bitacorn`**.

Earn `Bitacorn` from:
- Impromptu challenges
- Secret discovery bonuses
- Other event rewards

Spend `Bitacorn` to:
- Buy secrets
- Make deals
- Bid in the final star auction

---

## Final Star Auction

At the end of the event, teams bid `Bitacorn` for additional stars.

Twist: teams do **not** know how many auction rounds there will be.

Spend wisely.

---

## Spirit of the Game

- Investigate
- Make alliances
- Trade secrets
- Mislead opponents
- Have fun

You may be talking to a spy.

---

## Key Rules

- Secrets are locked once submitted on Day 1
- Only team captains may trigger fallback
- If no deal is reached within `10 minutes` of initial ask, fallback is required
- Fallback must be issued within that same `10-minute` window
- Fallback guess is final
- Only assigned secret targets are valid for submission that day

---

## Pro Tips

- Ask casually and early
- Compare fallback sets with allies
- Submit as soon as you are confident
- Save some `Bitacorn` for the auction
- Keep your team's official secret answers aligned internally
